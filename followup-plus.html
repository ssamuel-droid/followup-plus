One-time setup — about 3 minutes. After this, FollowUp+ runs automatically every morning.
1
Open Google Apps ScriptGo to script.google.com/create and sign in with the Gmail account you want to monitor.
2
Paste your scriptDelete all existing code in the editor. Go back to Step 3, click "Copy Script", then paste into the Apps Script editor. Save with Ctrl+S (Cmd+S on Mac).
3
Deploy as Web AppClick Deploy → New Deployment. Set type to Web App. Set "Execute as" to Me. Set "Who has access" to Anyone. Click Deploy and copy the Web App URL shown.
4
Paste the Web App URL into the scriptBack in the editor, find WEB_APP_URL: "PASTE_YOUR_WEB_APP_URL_HERE" near the top and replace the placeholder with the URL you just copied. Save again.
5
Run setupTriggerIn the function dropdown at the top of the editor, select setupTrigger and click Run. Accept the permissions Google requests — this is your own script accessing your own Gmail.
6
Test it immediatelySelect sendDigestNow and click Run. Check your inbox — your digest should arrive within seconds. If nothing shows up, all your recent sent emails have been replied to.
✅ To stop FollowUp+ at any time

Run the stopTrigger function in Apps Script. No more digest emails will be sent.

Stays in your Google account

The script lives entirely inside your own Google Apps Script. Your emails never leave Google's servers — nothing passes through any third-party service.

⚡

One-click follow-ups

Each digest email has a "Send Follow-Up" button per thread. One click and your personalized message is sent with the original email re-forwarded — instantly.

🛡️

Duplicate-proof & secure

Once a follow-up is sent the thread is labeled and excluded from future digests. SHA-256 tokens on every link prevent replay attacks and accidental double-sends.
